Astrology has been a spiritual and practical system for thousands of years, offering insights into personality, destiny, and the subtle forces that shape daily life. Among the systems in common use, Vedic astrology (Jyotish) is notable for its predictive techniques and precise planetary calculations.

What is Vedic Astrology?

Vedic astrology studies planetary positions at the time of birth and interprets their influence on different areas of life. Unlike Western astrology, which commonly uses the tropical zodiac, Vedic astrology typically uses the sidereal zodiac.
